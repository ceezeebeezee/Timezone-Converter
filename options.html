Timezone Converter

Settings

Used as the source timezone when the selected text doesn't include timezone information. Auto-detected from your system on first use.

Current shortcut: Ctrl+Shift+T (Windows/Linux) or Cmd+Shift+T (Mac)
Change it at chrome://extensions/shortcuts

1. Select text containing a date or time on any webpage.
2. Right-click and choose "Convert to another timezone", or press the keyboard shortcut.
3. Type a city name in the popup and press Enter.
4. The converted time is inserted right after your selection.
